# Brand Overview (Quick Ingest)

Premium Blends is a small-batch wellness brand focused on calm, clarity, and
responsible adult use. The brand emphasizes transparent sourcing, batch-level
testing, and clear guidance for new customers.

## Core pillars

- Quality: consistent, testable, and repeatable.
- Clarity: plain-language guidance with no medical claims.
- Community: local roots and customer education.

## Voice notes

- Grounded, practical, and supportive.
- Avoid hype or miracle language.
- Encourage starting low and going slow.
